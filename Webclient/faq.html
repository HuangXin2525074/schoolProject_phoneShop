Frequently Asked Questions

We're here to help.

The Company

  • What features can I expect on to find on your website?

    The website offers:

    • Log in/Sign Up as Customer or Visitor and view all the products and add to cart
    • User can view the products by choosing categories
    • User can search specific products
    • View the company details from home page
    • Track order

Products

  • Do you offer GST refund?
    No, we do not as we are not GST registered.
  • Are all the phones are unlocked?
    Singapore is the first country that forbids outright SIM locking thus all mobile phones are SIM-free or unlocked and can be use with any sim card compatible with our network.
  • Do you offer Installment plan?
    No, we do not offer any installment plan for our products.
  • How do I view products?
    You can view the prodcuts by signing up as a customer or as a visitor. If you already have an account, then you only have to log in.
  • Do the products come with any warranty?
    Yes, all items come with the respective manufacturer's local warranty.
  • How do I buy product(s)?
    After logged in as customer or visitor, user will be able to see list of products, from there user can choose the product(s) that want to buy and add to the cart. Cart will display all the products that added and display the total price.
  • How do I search products?
    After logged in as customer or visitor, at the top left corner of list of products, user can search by entering description of the product and pressing search.
  • How do I track my order?
    User will get unique confirmation number after submitting the order from cart, after that user can track their order by entering their confirmation number in the "TRACK YOUR ORDER" section in the Home page.

Account Page

  • How do I view account details?
    You can view the Account page by clicking the Menu button at the top right corner of the page and then selecting the Account button in the sidebar menu. You will then be redirected to the Account page where the details of your account are displayed. Clicking on the various detail tabs will load different categories of account information.
  • How do I edit account details?
    You can edit account details by clicking on the "Edit Profile" button which will redirect you to a form which contains all the fields for the account. To cancel making any changes and return to the basic Account page, click on "Return to View Mode".
  • How do I change my password?
    From the Account Page, click on the Change Password button. A popup form will be displayed for you to enter your current password, followed by the new password in two separate fields (these values must match).
  • How do I delete my account?
    You can delete your account by selecting the “Delete Profile” button from the Account page. A confirmation prompt will be displayed, and you will need to select "Yes" to delete the account.
